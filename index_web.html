Not everywhere burns.
Not everywhere breathes.

Hot and cold spots are defined by the convergence of four thermal indicators measured at 100m resolution across every urban pixel in Hessen. A hotspot is where heat accumulates during the day, lingers through the night, and translates into real physiological stress. A coldspot is where the landscape consistently keeps temperatures down.
